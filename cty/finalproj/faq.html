FAQs

Q: Why do you make smoothies and not other produce?

Smoothies is what made the Webber family's business into what it is today-- Jumpin' Juice Co. recognizes the importance of the delicious beverage!


Q: What goes into the smoothies Jumpin' Juice Co. makes?

Classic Oklahoma love, care and consideration-- as well as farm-fresh produce and recipes that twist to make your taste buds jump!


Q: Where does your produce come from?

All of our produce comes straight from local farmers, giving a chance for people just like Mr. Webber to be a part of something greater.


Q: Who runs Jumpin' Juice Co.?

Our brand is run on transparency. Isla Webber, the granddaughter of the legendary and locally renowned David Webber. She cares wholeheartedly about every customer!


Q: What makes Jumpin' Juice Co. so much different from other juice and smoothie companies?

We have the know-how from years of experience and none of the corporate undertones. We're not out for your wallets; we're just regular people who want to give other good people good drinks.
